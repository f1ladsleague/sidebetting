    Custom Betting Rules
    • How it works: Propose a clear TRUE or FALSE statement about an upcoming race and set a wager. Other bettors can accept your bet by taking the opposite side.
    • The Pot & Payouts: The total money wagered by both sides creates the Pot. If your side wins, you split the Pot proportionally with anyone else who bet the same way as you, minus a flat 10% League Fee.
    • Valid Bets: If only one side of the statement is bet on (e.g., people bet TRUE but nobody bets FALSE), the bet is considered INVALID and all wagers are returned.
