Rules & Regulations

  • Public Guests: Welcome to the Paddock! Enter your name and email address to place bets and track your Net W/L.
  • How does it work?: To participate, select one driver for the Sprint and/or Feature race to enter. The amount next to their name is the buy-in amount you are required to pay to play. If your driver wins, you split the total buy-in pot evenly with any other winning bettors after a flat 10% League Fee is deducted. If you lose, you must pay the buy-in amount to the league. For e.g., You pay $10 to bet that Driver A wins the Sprint Race. The total pot for the Sprint Race is $100 (including your $10). If Driver A wins, and you are one of two people who backed Driver A to win, the $90 remaining after the league's $10 cut is split so you receive a $45 payout (meaning you are up by $35).
  • League Fee: The League takes a flat 10% fee on all side bet winnings.
  • Selection Limit: You can only select ONE driver for the Sprint and/or ONE driver for the Feature race.
  • Deadlines: Bets lock strictly at 11:59 PM the day before the race weekend begins.
